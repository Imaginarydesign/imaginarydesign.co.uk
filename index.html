Why hire me

I love a challenge; the more complex the better. For me there is nothing more rewarding than taking a complicated problem, breaking it down and delivering a beautifully simple solution. I question everything, continually asking “can this be better?”

Over the past 15 years I’ve worked as both a designer and a front-end developer. Projects have included small websites for local businesses right through to multimillion pound e-commerce applications.

  • I have designed, redesigned, custom built and helped to launch multiple websites and web apps.
  • I've created brand identities and guidelines, logos, stationery, brochures, newsletters and internal reports.
  • I’ve developed and helped to maintain internal design systems and interface design guidelines.
  • I’ve contributed to streamlining work-flows and internal processes.
  • I've helped to improve the working relationships between designers and developers.

Ultimately, my work is all about helping businesses bring their digital product and service ideas to life and making them easy to use. I love making beautiful visuals but this is not what drives me. What drives me is the underlying user need. I want to create something that actually solves user problems.

FAQ

Do you code?

I believe in goals, not tools but I always tinker with code and explore ways how technology can help us be more efficient and have better lives.

What tools do you use?

Safari is my main browser. I use Figma and Principle for all my UI work, Sublime Text 3 for when I am editing a code and iTerm2 when interacting with a command line. I use Todoist to organise my tasks and BearApp for writing. I use git and Github/Bitbucket for code versioning.
